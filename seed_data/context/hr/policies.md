# Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full-time remote arrangements require partner approval. Client-facing days must be in-office or on-site with the client.

## Expense Policy

- Client entertainment: up to €150/person, requires receipt
- Travel: economy class for flights under 4 hours, business class for longer
- Hotels: up to €200/night in major cities, €150 elsewhere
- All expenses over €500 require pre-approval from managing partner

## Leave Policy

- Annual leave: 25 days
- Sick leave: unlimited with medical certificate after 3 consecutive days
- Parental leave: 16 weeks (birth parent), 4 weeks (other parent)
- Notice period: 2 weeks for planned leave, none for sick leave

## Confidentiality

All client information is strictly confidential. Client data must not be shared between engagement teams without explicit client consent. See NDA template in legal/templates/.
